About us About us

Moley's Sugarrush by ana was founded as result of the aftermath of World War 2. We started as a web design studio intended to create digital media content. Highlighting a theme to please user's eyes with colorful and fun designs-to somehow delight the depressed people that lost their loved ones. We thought this was the right thing to do. Not long after, Ana - the head of our Baking and Kitchen Division, suddenly converted the whole studio into a bake shop that "should sells pastries, sweets, and baked goods."

Two years after that, she still hasn't baked anything so I started making logos and rebrand the whole Sugarrush several times. I did that for two years. And until now, as we speak, I am rebranding Sugarrush a new logo and look.

I don't know when she will start to bake.
